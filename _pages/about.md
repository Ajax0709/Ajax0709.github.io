Hi, I am Ajax, a second-year M.S.E. student in Computer and Information Science at the University of Pennsylvania.

My research interests broadly lie in operating systems, large language models, and AI for systems, with a growing focus on distributed systems. I am particularly interested in how system design, scheduling, and runtime mechanisms can be optimized to better support modern workloads.

Currently, I am working on scheduling research with [Suwei Ma](https://willma17.github.io/), under the guidance of Professors [Sebastian Angel](https://www.cis.upenn.edu/~sga001/) and Professor [Linh Thi Xuan Phan](https://www.cis.upenn.edu/~linhphan/). Previously, I had my summer internship in the Cloud and AI Group at Microsoft Research Asia, where I conducted research on fine-tuning Qwen-VL and benchmarking for multimodal LLMs.

I received my undergraduate degree from the College of William & Mary, where I double majored in Computer Science and Economics and had the privilege of being advised by Professor [Qun Li](https://www.cs.wm.edu/~liqun/).

Please don't hesitate to reach out if you would like to connect, collaborate, or just chat!
